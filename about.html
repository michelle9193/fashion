WHO ARE WE

We are a local company based in the Beautiful Mother City, Cape Town. We specialize in the revival of old things lost and new-old things gained. With our a passion for the environment and want to lessen our carbon footprint, we had the epiphany to do some research into the fashion market and that's how we came up with a "Fresh Take on the 90s".

Since 90s fashion made a comeback, and will not be leaving us anytime soon, we decided to hunt down all the trends of the past and shed some light on them in our current era. We realised that clothing was being manufactured on an enormous scale and we needed to play our part in being more mindful to our future generations.
